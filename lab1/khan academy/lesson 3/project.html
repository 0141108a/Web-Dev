Grilled Chicken Salad

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 200g
Lettuce 1 cup

Steps:

Step 1: Grill the chicken until cooked through.

Step 2: Chop lettuce and other veggies.

Step 3: Slice chicken and mix with vegetables.

Step 4: Add dressing and serve.

Vegetable Stir Fry

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Broccoli 1 cup
Carrots 1 cup

Steps:

Step 1: Heat oil in a wok or pan.

Step 2: Add broccoli and carrots, stir fry 5-7 minutes.

Step 3: Add soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 4: Serve hot with rice or noodles.
